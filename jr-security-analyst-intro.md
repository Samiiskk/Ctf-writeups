## Role — Jr. Security Analyst (SOC Level 1)
- First responder to security alerts
- Works in a 24/7 SOC team
- Reviews and triages alerts with colleagues

## Daily Duties
- Monitor and investigate security alerts
- Participate in SOC brainstorms and workshops
- Cooperate with other teams
- Constantly learn new attacks and defenses

## SOC Team Members
| Person | Role | What they do |
|--------|------|-------------|
| Senior Analyst | Will Griffin type | Helps with complex cases after L1 triage |
| SOC Engineer | Corey Stevens type | Maintains tools, configures alerts |
| SOC Manager | Emily Conway type | Reports to management, keeps team on track |
| Incident Responder | Daniel Herrera type | Called in during major incidents only |

## Real World Scenarios a Jr. Analyst faces
- Detecting data stealer infection on a laptop
- Stopping a phishing campaign on finance team
- Participating in ransomware attack response
- Building detection rules with teammates

## Practical Lab
- Analyzed alert dashboard for malicious IP
- Escalated alert to senior analyst
- Blocked malicious IP on firewall

## Key Takeaway
The job is challenging — busy shifts with many tickets —
but rewarding when you stop a real threat.
SOC Level 1 is the entry point into cybersecurity defense.
